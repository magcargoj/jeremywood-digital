# The "Win-Win" List: Trial-Based Remote Companies
**Goal:** Land a "Paid Trial" while still at BlueTone. Earn double pay while proving your value in a nomad-native environment.

---

### 🟢 Tier 1: The "Gold Standard" Trials (2-4 Weeks)
These companies have formalized trials that are long enough to feel like a "real" job and pay very well.

| Company | Role Type | Trial Format | Why it's a "Win" |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Automattic** | Happiness/VIP Eng | 2-8 Weeks (Paid) | They invented the model. You work alongside your current job. |
| **Toggl** | Support / Product | 1-Week "Test Drive" | 40 hours of paid work to see if you can handle their async culture. |
| **10up** | AdOps / Support | Project-Based | Great for you because they are a huge WordPress agency (High demand for AdOps). |

---

### 🟡 Tier 2: The "Test Project" Sprints (2-5 Days)
These companies use shorter, highly-focused paid engagements (usually 10-20 hours).

| Company | Role Type | Trial Format | Pay Structure |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Buffer** | Support Hero | "Task Week" | Paid for a series of real tickets/scenarios. |
| **Hotjar** | Support / Ops | "Task Day" | $300 - $500 for a deep-dive technical project. |
| **Doist** | Support / Product | "Test Project" | A flat fee for a specific problem-solving deliverable. |
| **Baremetrics** | Customer Success | Paid Trial | Focuses on your ability to analyze customer churn data. |

---

### 🔵 Tier 3: The "Freelance-to-Full-time" Gateways
These platforms vet you first, then set you up for "Trials" with high-end clients.

*   **Toptal:** If you pass their screening, you are often placed in "trials" with enterprise clients (like Airbnb or Shopify). These are high-pay ($80+/hr) and often lead to full-time remote retainers.
*   **X-Team:** They specialize in "Scaling Remote Teams." They hire you as an engineer/support lead and place you in paid projects with massive global brands.

---

### 🛡️ The Jeremy "Double Pay" Protocol
Since you work the overnight shift at BlueTone, you have a unique advantage: **The Daylight Advantage.**

1.  **Overnight Shift (12am - 8am):** Do your BlueTone work as normal.
2.  **Nap/Family (8am - 12pm):** Rest and engage with the kids.
3.  **Trial Sprint (12pm - 4pm):** Work your 4 hours for Automattic or Toggl.
4.  **The Result:** You collect your full BlueTone salary **PLUS** your ~$25/hr trial pay. This builds your "Emergency Fund" even faster before you fly to Guatemala.
