# Portfolio Domain & Branding Strategy

To secure a $100k+ IC3/IC4 role at a company like Zapier or Affirm, your domain name needs to scream **"Qualified Technical Architect"** the moment it hits their browser address bar. 

### 1. The "Silicon Valley Preferred" (Highly Recommended)
These extensions are the standard for SaaS engineering and implementation teams.
*   **jeremywood.dev** — *The definitive choice.* It tells the recruiter you are part of the developer ecosystem.
*   **jeremywood.io** — *The "Startup" choice.* Common for Solutions Engineers and Architects.
*   **wood.tech** — Short, punchy, and modern.
