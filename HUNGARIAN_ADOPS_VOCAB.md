# Hungarian Tech & AdOps Vocabulary (A1 Level)

Because we put "Hungarian (A1 / Beginner Study)" on your resume specifically to flag recruiters at Prezi and GoTo, you need to be able to drop a few contextual words if an interviewer happens to be Hungarian or asks about it. 

You don't need to be fluent. You just need to show that you are *actually studying* and understand basic tech vocabulary in their language.

## 1. The Icebreakers
Use these if the interviewer mentions they are from the Budapest office.

*   **Szia! (SEE-yah)** - Hello/Hi (Informal, good for tech environments).
*   **Jó reggelt! (YOH reg-gelt)** - Good morning!
*   **Hogy vagy? (Hodge vadj)** - How are you?
*   **Köszönöm (KØ-sze-nøm)** - Thank you.
*   **Magyarul tanulok (MADJ-ar-ul TAW-nu-lok)** - I am learning Hungarian.

## 2. AdOps & Tech Cheat Sheet
If you seamlessly drop one of these when discussing why you want to transition, they will instantly believe you are serious about the language and the culture.

*   **Adatok (AW-daw-tok)** - Data
    *   *Mnemonic:* "I need a-dat-token of data."
*   **Hiba (HEE-baw)** - Error / Bug
    *   *Usage Check:* "I love tracking down a *hiba* in the frontend."
*   **Kód (KODE)** - Code
*   **Rendszer (REND-ser)** - System
*   **Optimalizálás (Op-ti-ma-li-ZAA-laash)** - Optimization

## 3. The Interview "Flex" Sentence
If an interviewer asks, "So why are you learning Hungarian?" 

**Your Answer:**
> "My family and I are actually planning a long-term transition to Central Europe, so I am actively building my vocabulary. Right now my main focus is on technical terms. Instead of just learning how to order a beer, I'm trying to learn words I'll use at work, like *adatok* (data) and *rendszer* (system). It's challenging but incredibly motivating!"

This proves your intent, hits the cultural touchstone, and perfectly aligns you for remote work with European-tied companies.
