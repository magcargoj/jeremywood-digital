# LinkedIn Profile Optimization Guide

To support your transition into a Technical AdOps or Implementation role, your LinkedIn profile needs to mirror the "unicorn" messaging of your new resume. Recruiter searches are heavily based on keyword indexing. We need to strip out "Service Director" fluff and highlight your technical operations expertise.

Copy and paste the sections below directly into your LinkedIn profile.

---

## 1. The Headline
Your headline is the most important part of your profile. It’s the first thing recruiters see, and it serves as the SEO metadata for LinkedIn’s search engine.

**Current/Old (Likely):** Service Director at BlueTone Media
**New (Use exactly this):** 
> Technical Implementation & AdOps Specialist | Google Tag Manager (GTM) | Web Analytics & Conversion Tuning | CSM & PMP

---

## 2. The "About" Section (Summary)
This section should highlight your ability to bridge the gap between pure web development and revenue operations.

**Copy/Paste this:**
> I am a technical implementation and digital operations specialist focused on the precise intersection of web development and ad-tech. With over 6 years of experience, I specialize in auditing front-end architectures, deploying flawless conversion tracking (GTM, Meta, GA4), and ensuring exact delivery pacing for high-volume digital environments.
>
> My background as a web developer paired with my certifications (PMP, CSM) allows me to operate as a high-level Individual Contributor. I bypass traditional tier-1 roadblocks by using Chrome DevTools, raw HTML/JS analysis, and direct backend script injection to resolve complex tracking anomalies.
>
> I am currently seeking a 100% remote Technical Operations, AdOps, or Implementation role where I can trade macro-management for deep technical precision, flawless execution, and data accuracy.

---

## 3. The "Experience" Updates
Update your current and recent roles. If you want to list "Service Director," that's fine for the timeline, but change the displayed title or clarify your technical impact.

### **Current Role**
**Title:** Technical Support & Implementation Specialist *(or Technical Operations Lead)*
**Company:** BlueTone Media
**Description:**
> • Serve as the technical anchor for digital marketing operations, resolving complex tracking, hosting, and API integration anomalies across dozens of high-traffic sites.

> • Execute complex backend ticket resolutions independently, bypassing tier-1 support to deploy database optimizations and script injections natively.
> • Process high-volume, data-heavy task queues with meticulous accuracy, continuously hitting 100% SLA compliance for ad-tech deployment and issue resolution.

*(Optional: List your Director title underneath this, but use the bullet points from the new resume emphasizing technical workflows and CRO).*

## 5. "Open to Work" Settings
Set your backend recruiter preferences to match your July 15th Central America logic:

1. **Job Titles:** Ad Operations Specialist, Technical Implementation Specialist, Revenue Operations, Technical Support Engineer.
2. **Workplaces:** Remote ONLY.
3. **Locations:** (If asked) United States. (Remember, even if you are in Central America, you are a US remote worker).
4. **Visibility:** Set to **Recruiters Only** so your current employer doesn’t get notified.
