# LinkedIn AdOps Networking Templates

Applying cold via job boards is exactly what we just did, but to guarantee an interview, you have to bypass the HR filter. These templates are designed for you to copy, paste, and send to AdOps Managers, Directors of Revenue Operations, or Implementation Leads at your target companies.

They are designed to take advantage of your "Unicorn" positioning.

---

## 1. The "Current Tech Fixer" Approach (Use for Dotdash / Future PLC)
*Target: "Director of Ad Operations" or "Senior Technical Account Manager"*

**Subject / Connection Note:**
> AdOps / Technical Support Sync

**Message:**
> Hi [Name],
> 
> I’m currently the technical anchor for a digital agency resolving high-volume GTM, GA4, and pixel integration anomalies for our portfolio. I saw [Company] is scaling their operational team, and I really respect how your properties handle ad-tech complexity. 
> 
> I’m actively pursuing remote IC roles where I can bring absolute DevTools precision to an established ops team. Would you be open to a quick 5-minute chat about the technical hurdles your deployment team is facing this quarter?
> 
> Best,
> Jeremy Wood
